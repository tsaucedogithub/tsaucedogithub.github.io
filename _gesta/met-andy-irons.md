Me getting Andy Irons's autograph. This was one of his last major surf contests before his untimely death, which was honestly more influential on me than I'd have thought. I still think about it. When he died I rewatched his ["I surf because"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4uwtqRBE4Kk) short film over and over.
