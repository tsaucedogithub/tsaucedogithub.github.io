My second time graduating Stanford. Despite completing my Classics minor the year before, I decided to walk with the Classics department. This was the correct decision. It was much smaller, more intimate, and fun, and I knew the people there. The CS commencement was so large that, once the Classics one finished, I left, snuck into the back of the line, and walked with my CS friends as well. If I had to choose, I'd choose Classics every time.
