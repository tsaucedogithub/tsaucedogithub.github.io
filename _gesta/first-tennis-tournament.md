I played in my first tennis tournament ever. I began playing tennis when I moved to San Francisco because a lot of my friends from college who lived in SF played tennis. I played in this tournament with a good friend of mine, Max, who lives in the South Bay, whose racket I also used. Note that I am not short; he is just 6'6".

In the tournament, I won my first match in a tiebreaker, and my second match, I lost 6-0 against someone who actually knew how to play. Though I can lull myself to sleep by reminding myself that I play tennis for fun and not to win, which is what I will say until I win some random tennis tournament.
